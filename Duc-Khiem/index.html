CHANEL Fine Jewelry creations are inspired by the daring expressed in Mademoiselle Chanel’s first jewelry collection in 1932, as well as the strong themes that symbolize the brand’s identity. Between 1988 and 2007, Lorenz Bäumer designed collections of High Jewelry and Fine Jewelry for CHANEL. To discover these pieces, please click here. Since then, the Maison has developed its own Studio of Creation. From the first sketches inspired by the world of Gabrielle Chanel, to the detailed artistic rendering of a jewelry piece destined for the workshops, the Studio of Creation forever reinvents the world of CHANEL Fine Jewelry.
